What is Aapka Online Store?

Aapka Online Store is an e-store platform that enables you to have your very own online, personalized and branded e-store to showcase your Products and/or Services on the web at an extremely reasonable price.

Unlike other e-store platform service providers, Aapka Online Store takes full responsibility of the initial set up of your e-store. Yes, you are right, we will set up your store for you. All you have to do is “Just Display and Sell your Products”.

Your e-store will be an exact replica of our demo store but with Your Logo, Your Products and/or Services and Your Personal Subdomain (i.e. yourcompanyname.aapkaonlinestore.com).

Features

Mobile Optimized

Our platform is optimized for all types of mobile devices. Your store visitors can now conveniently shop via their mobile devices.

Payment Options

Configure COD, Cheque and NEFT. as Payment Options for all the Products and or Services that you intend to display and sell on your e-store. All these payment options shall be visible on the mobile version of your store as well.

Social Media Sharing

Enable your e-store visitors to share your Product and/or Service on social media with just a single click.

Smart Product Filters

Enable your visitors to seamlessly filter within the range of your products/services and zero in on the product of their choice.

Video Testimonials

Embed Customer Video Testimonials right next to your Product and/or Service so that your e-store visitors can make an informed decision before making a purchase.

Email and SMS Alerts

Get instant e-mail and SMS alerts every time you receive an order from your e-store. Also, an automatic email notification is sent to your customers about the receipt of their order and also after their order is processed and shipped.

Company Logo and Branding

It’s your e-store. And we firmly believe it should have your logo and your branding message.

Reports and Analytics

Generate reports and gain remarkable insights about the performance of your e-store.

Stock Management

Manage Product Stock efficiently and commit to delivery timelines accordingly.

Facebook Store

Display your store right from within your Facebook Business Page.
